About Us

Lanes Recruitment was established with a vision to not only acknowledge the clients requirements but also help and assist our drivers progress through their careers whilst understanding each individuals personal working requirements. Lanes Recruitment have teamed up with active and retired professionals, ranging from HGV drivers to Motor Vehicle Technicians to allow us to deliver an efficient and safe service. LR offers its clients and candidates with flexible employment opportunities, including temporary and full time work as well as fixed term contracts.

We aim to have an honest and transparent relationship with both clients and candidates, providing constructive feedback when necessary. Whilst Lanes Recruitment appreciates the current market we also provide our candidates with an ‘on the go App’ to view, book and confirm jobs while also creating a personal profile, to update availability without the need to call.
